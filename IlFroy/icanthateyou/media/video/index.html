CHRONICLE ENTRY — The Kindness That Refused To Harden
How does the heart decide to stay soft when it has every reason to harden?

I cannot hate you, even knowing that loving me is something you cannot give, because what I feel was never built on conditions or outcomes. I love you so much that it exists quietly, without resentment, without demands, simply present.

There are days when I wonder how affection can survive absence, how care can remain gentle when it is not returned, yet here I am, still choosing tenderness.

Do you know how deeply you are etched into me, how even disappointment cannot erase the warmth I hold for you?

My love does not ask you to change or explain yourself. It only exists, steady and sincere, accepting the truth while still honoring what my heart feels, because some love is meant to endure, not to be answered.
