some people
are only meant
to be memories

I built this place because I thought love was something that could stay. Something that could survive distance, silence, fear, timing, confusion, and two people slowly becoming strangers while still pretending nothing changed.

But not every story ends with understanding. Some end with unread messages, empty tabs, disappearing routines, and the quiet realization that you were holding on alone.

I waited longer than I should have. I replayed conversations until they lost meaning. I searched for signs that were probably never there. And somewhere along the way, I stopped asking whether they loved me back and started asking whether I was allowed to let go.

Maybe love is not always about staying. Maybe sometimes it is about accepting that someone changed your life, even if they never stayed long enough to see what they became to you.

So this is the last page. No dramatic ending. No hatred. No revenge. Just someone finally closing the door after standing there for too long.

goodbye, froylan_
